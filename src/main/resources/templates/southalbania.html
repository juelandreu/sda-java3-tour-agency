5 Days Albanian Exotics Riviera and Gjirokstra Tour Price Starting From 300€

With its jumble of rugged mountains fringed by pristine curls of beach, Albania’s south is the most appealing part of the country. The interior route boasts the rewarding towns of Beratiand Gjirokastra, each home to whole swathes of Ottoman buildings. Heading on down the Ionian Coast instead, you’ll find one of Europe’s only unspoilt sections of Mediterranean shore, a near-permanently sunny spot where the twin blues of sea and sky are ripped asunder by a ribbon of grey mountains – on a clear day you’ll be able to see Italy from the 1027m-high Llogaraja Pass. Both routes converge at the beach town of Saranda, while further south are the fantastic ruins of Butrinti.

Itinerary

Day 1

Tirana – Vlora Meet and assist with the guide at the meeting point. Have a short, walking tour in the main city center with your tour guide. You will visit the main sites, starting from; Skanderbeg square to the main boulevard and the administrative areas. You will spend in Tirana around 2 hours and then you will depart to Vlora. Arrive in Vlora and after check –in, have lunch at a traditional & nice restaurant. After lunch, the guide will show you the city. Vlora was the first capital in Albania and also the city where was declared the independence of Albania in 1912. You will have the chance to visit the waterfront and the square of the flag, the museum of the independence with the assist of the guide. You will have your own free time for relaxation near the Adriatic Sea and dinner on your own. Your guide will suggest you the best restaurants because he knows very well this region! Let him lead you to this wonderful tour. Dinner and Overnight in Vlora.

Day 2

National Park of Llogara After breakfast, start your morning with a walk along the beach. After that you will depart to the National Park of llogara, which lasts1 hour away from Vlora. Driving by the Adriatic seaside will be an amazing experience. After driving you will go up in the mountain that is nearby the seashore. Arrival in Llogara and have lunch first. After lunch start the walking tour in the National Park trails. You will spend around 2 hours walking and enjoying the natural beauty of this park. There is a chance of hiking in the National Park. You will go back at hotel and then have some the free time in the Park. The Llogara Pass is always a high point (literally) on any tour of Albania. We’ll climb up from sea level to an altitude of 1,000 meters in about 20 minutes, enjoying magnificent views of the coast along the way (and yes…we’ll stop for a photo.) You will spend the night in the fantastic park. Dinner and Overnight in the National Park.

Day 3

Kayak Experience in Drimadhes After breakfast meet the driver and your guide at the entrance of hotel and depart to the stunning Albanian Riviera. You will enjoy this amazing, panoramic view from the mountain to the Ionian seaside. Arrival on the beach of Drimadhes by relaxing near Ionian Sea. Take the Kayak and transfer to the amazing beach of Gjipe (in Gjipe beach you can only go by off-road cars or Kayak). After enjoying your Kayak experience on the Ionian seaside, prepare for the lunch. You will enjoy the seafood and fresh puddings besides the beach. After lunch have a short walk to explore the canyon of Gjipe. Free time for relaxation in the crystal waters of the seaside. After relaxation visit have free time for your dinner (restaurant suggested by your guide). Overnight in Himara Beach

Day 4

Himara – Sarande After breakfast depart to Sarande. Visit the picturesque Gulf of Porto Palermo and the small castle built by Ali Pasha Tepelena on an attractive island, built in 1807 -1808. Himara, Qeparo and Borsh are traditional villages known for the cultivation of citrus fruits and where the olives’ landscapes, combined with the charming villages. Enjoy Himara nearby the clear waters. Have lunch in Himare. Free time nearby the seaside. Don’t miss the sunset because you will miss the heaven’s show. Dinner and overnight in Saranda.

Day 5

Saranda – Gjirokastra – Tirana Leave the hotel and drive to the ancient town of Gjirokstra . Arrival in Gjirokastra and you will the the history of Albania. The historic site in Gjirokastra, the “Neck of the Bazaar”, makes up the center of the Old Town but what were once artisanal workshops are today mainly tourist souvenir shops. There are a couple of stone carvers left at the top end of the bazaar. Before dinner time you will have free time in the bazaar. visit:Interior: Gjirokastra castle, Skendulaj fortress Gjirokastra house, part of which has been transformed into a political prison for dissidents of the regime and the weapons museum. Visit the Ethnographic Museum, which is also the house in which the Albanian dictator was born, as well as a 300-year-old house next door, where the greatness of Gjirokastra’s master builders can be seen. After visit have some free and lunch. Transfer back to Tirana. This will be the end of our service.
